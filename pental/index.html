Pental

A tactical battle game in five dimensions where the board is bigger than what you can see.

A turn-based strategy game set in five-dimensional space.

Five unique creatures fight for survival across a world bigger than what you can see at once.

Pental is a tactical battle game built around a simple but ambitious idea: what if a game board had five dimensions instead of three? You always see a 3D cube, but movement into unseen dimensions smoothly rotates your view to reveal new layers of the space, like turning a corner you didn't know was there.

How it plays

Each match features five creature types, each with their own stat profile and signature ability. Choose your fighter, outmaneuver your opponents across dimensions they can't see, and be the last one standing.
