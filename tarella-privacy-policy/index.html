Overview

Tarella Notes is designed with your privacy in mind. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data Collection

Tarella Notes does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. All notes you create are stored locally on your device and never leave it. There are no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Third-Party Services

Tarella Notes does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Tarella Notes does not collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last Updated" date.
