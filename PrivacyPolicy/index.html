Privacy Policy for Tarella Notes

Tarella Notes is a simple, personal note-taking app designed to store information locally on your device. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Data Collection

Tarella Notes does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All notes created in the app are saved locally on your device and are not sent to any server.

Data Sharing

Since no data is collected, nothing is shared with third parties.

Analytics & Tracking

Tarella Notes does not use any analytics tools, ad networks, or third-party tracking.

Device Permissions

The app does not request access to your location, camera, microphone, or contacts.
