Warframe
Price Checker

Look up current market prices for Warframe items and mods.

The quick, in-game price checker for Warframe relic rewards.

WFPC is a lightweight desktop tool that reads what's on your screen and tells you what it's worth. No alt-tabbing, no manual searching, no guesswork.

When you're staring at a relic reward screen, WFPC captures a slice of your screen, reads the item names using OCR, and instantly pulls the current best buy price from warframe.market. You get real numbers in seconds so you can make smarter picks and better trades before you're sent back to your orbiter.

How it works

Hit the screenshot button, and WFPC handles the rest. It reads the item text on screen, matches it against every known prime item on the market, and shows you what buyers are currently paying. No copy-pasting item names, no keeping a browser tab open on the side.

Built with

Tesseract OCR for reading item names, OpenCV for image processing, and the warframe.market API for live pricing data. Runs locally on your machine.

WFPC was made for the moments in Warframe where you need a price and you need it now, without leaving the game to go find it.
