Price Checker

Look up current market prices for Warframe items and mods.

A quick price checker for relic mission rewards.

WFPC is a desktop overlay that sits on top of your game, reads the relic reward screen, and tells you what everything is worth. No alt-tabbing, no manual searching, no guesswork.

Position the transparent window over your reward choices, hit Capture, and WFPC does the rest. It reads the item names using OCR, matches them against every known prime item on warframe.market, and lays out the current best buy prices right there in the overlay. Individual part prices, full set prices, and which option puts the most platinum in your pocket, all visible before you have to pick.

How it works

WFPC stays pinned above your game with a see-through capture region. When rewards appear, it grabs a screenshot of what's behind the overlay, processes the text with OCR, and pulls live pricing data from warframe.market. Results show up in card-style panels, one per reward, with part breakdowns and set comparisons highlighted so the best pick stands out.

Built with

Tesseract OCR and OpenCV for screen reading and image processing. The warframe.market API for live pricing. Tkinter for the overlay UI. Runs locally on Windows.

WFPC was made for the moments in Warframe where you need a price and you need it now, without leaving the game to go find it.
