Tarella
Notes

A simple, private note-taking app for iOS. Your notes stay on your device, always.

The warm, no-fuss notes app that keeps your thoughts organized and always within reach.

Tarella Notes is a personal note-taking app that makes it easy to capture what matters, find it when you need it, and keep everything tidy without thinking too hard about it.

Whether you're jotting down daily thoughts, tracking ongoing projects, or just keeping notes you don't want to lose, Tarella is a calm, reliable place for all of it.

What you can do

  • Create notes with a title, date, and as much or as little detail as you like
  • Browse notes automatically sorted into Today, Last 7 Days, and Older
  • Search instantly across titles, tags, and content
  • Edit any note after the fact, with automatic tracking of when it was last updated
  • Glance at a simple home screen that shows how many notes you've saved and how many days you've written notes

Private by design

Everything stays on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no fuss.

Tarella Notes was made for people who just want a notes app that feels good to use. Simple, personal, and always ready when you are.
